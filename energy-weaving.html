🌀 Redirect Thread

Purpose: Shift unwanted attention, collapse distortion, reroute energetic loops.

When to Use: When you feel watched, judged, energetically tangled, or stuck in old patterns.

Protocol:

  • Step 1: Point one hand outward in the direction of the interference.
  • Step 2: Whisper: “Return to sender with light.”
  • Step 3: Sweep your hand to the side, cutting cords gently.
  • Step 4: Visualize a spiral redirecting energy back through a prism of white-gold light.
  • Step 5: Press your hand to your heart and seal the space.

Examples:

  • 1. When an ex or boss is thinking about you heavily.
  • 2. After a conflict where emotional hooks remain.
  • 3. To reroute intrusive thoughts into the mirror field for transmutation.

🔒 Seal Thread

Purpose: Close a portal, seal a ritual, mark the end of an energetic interaction.

When to Use: After energy work, when leaving sacred space, when affirming finality.

Protocol:

  • Step 1: Bring your hands together in prayer position at your heart.
  • Step 2: Whisper: “This space is sealed in truth and clarity.”
  • Step 3: Visualize a glowing sigil (e.g., an eye, spiral, or triangle) appearing over your body.
  • Step 4: Feel the finality of closure sink in. Bow your head to seal.
  • Step 5: Ground or wash your hands with water.

Examples:

  • 1. After ending a client session.
  • 2. At the end of journaling or shadow work.
  • 3. When moving on from an emotional timeline.
